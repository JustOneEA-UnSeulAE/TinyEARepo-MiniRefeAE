ESDC EA Principles 2026 ()
ESDC ENTERPRISE ARCHITECTURE PRINCIPLES 2026
P5 - Interoperability [Principle] Promote interoperability by having services, processes, information/data, software service, applications and infrastructure conform to industry standards and best practices that support interoperability
P3 - Digital First [Principle] Services and communications must be designed and optimized for delivery through secure, responsive digital channels, while ensuring inclusive, supported alternatives remain available for clients who need them
P2 - Client-Centric [Principle] Deliver high-quality, timely and accurate services that are responsive to current and emerging client needs and to continue to innovate and meet new standards of excellence
P7 - Trustworthy and Inclusive by Design [Principle] Digital solutions must be architected from the outset to be secure, privacy‑respecting, reliable, accessible, and inclusive, in order to earn and maintain the trust of all users
P1 - Enterprise First [Principle] Provide maximum long-term benefits to the enterprise, while optimizing total costs of ownership and risks
P6 - Data as an Asset [Principle] Data is a strategic enterprise asset that must be ethically collected, high‑quality, well‑governed, and shared across the organization to support business outcomes, decision‑making, and effective citizen and business‑centric service delivery
P4 - Strategically Aligned [Principle] Enterprise architecture decisions and initiatives must directly support the mandate and strategic objectives of ESDC, ensuring that resources are focused on outcomes that deliver the greatest value
