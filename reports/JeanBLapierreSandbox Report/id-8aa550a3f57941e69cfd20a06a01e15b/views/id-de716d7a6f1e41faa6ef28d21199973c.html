The Enterprise Data Foundation Platform (EDFP) is a key component of the ESDC Data Strategy, delivering on all four pillars of the strategy, with a focus on infrastructure.
The EDFP enables secure , structured, and scalable, secure access to high-quality data, fostering innovation and supporting evidence-based decision-making through a robust suite of data services and platform components.
