BDM Data Strategy ()
PowerPoint Deck - Operational Data Platform DAB [Resource]
One Vision - Guided by ESDC and BDM data principles, enabling seamless benefits delivery by treating data as a shared, protected asset - driving responsive services, organizational agility, and a culture of stewardship and collaboration to improve outcomes for all Canadians [Driver]
1. Enable and accelerate data-driven decision-making with real-time and historical analytics, to drive continuous service improvement across BDM programs [Goal]
2. Safeguard and manage the data in a way that ensures that personal and corporate information remains secure and clients' rights to privacy are respected [Goal]
1.1 Integrate data across programs to be able to offer new or improved services such as “Tell Us Once” [Outcome]
1.2 Apply Machine Learning to assess risk and predict errors and fraud [Outcome]
1.3 Use Artificial Intelligence to help employees and clients find information and complete required tasks [Outcome]
2.1 Protect personal data by keeping it in operational systems and platforms; de-personalize data before transferring it to analytics environments [Outcome]
2.3 Reduce the time required to access data, add new sources, build pipelines, and generate insights [Outcome]
1.4 Generate reports and dashboards efficiently using accurate, high-quality, and reliable data [Outcome]
2.2 Offer consent-based services that let clients choose between protecting individual privacy and having access to convenient or shared services [Outcome]
