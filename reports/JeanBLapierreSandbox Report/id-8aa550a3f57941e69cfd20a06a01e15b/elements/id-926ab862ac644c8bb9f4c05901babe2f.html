Data Sharing (Business Function) ()
Data Sharing Collaborating with partners across the Department, Government of Canada (e.g. Statistics Canada or the Canada Revenue Agency) and other stakeholders outside the GC to get the right data, into the right hands, at the right time to support work across the policy to service continuum (e.g., planning, policy analysis, research, program and service delivery, monitoring, evaluation, or reporting). Links to processes and agreements that ensure ethical and responsible use of data, and the protection and security of client information.
