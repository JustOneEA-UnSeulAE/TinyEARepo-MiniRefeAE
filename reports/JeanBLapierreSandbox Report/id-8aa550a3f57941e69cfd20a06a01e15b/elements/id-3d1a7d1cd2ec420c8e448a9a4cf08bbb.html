P4 - Data Is Secure and Protected by Design Description Privacy, security, legal, and ethical considerations must be embedded by design and by default throughout the data lifecycle, from collection to disposal. Rationale Canadians entrust ESDC with sensitive information, and the Strategy explicitly links public trust to strong governance, privacy, security, and ethical use of data. Impacts • Architecture patterns must incorporate privacy-by-design and security-by-design controls. • Data access follows a “need to know” and role-based access model. • AI and advanced analytics require documented risk and impact assessments. • Vendor tools and platforms are subject to data-focused risk evaluation.
