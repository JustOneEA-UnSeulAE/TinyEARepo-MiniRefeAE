P7 - Data Enables Evidence-Informed and Human-Centric Decisions Description Data must be used to support evidence-informed decision-making, with a human-centric focus that respects rights, inclusivity, and fairness. Rationale The Strategy frames data use as a means to tailor services, improve outcomes, and ensure no one is left behind, particularly vulnerable and underserved populations. Impacts • Analytics and reporting must link data to meaningful outcomes and impacts. • EDI considerations must be embedded in data design and use. • Decisions relying on automated or analytical outputs require human oversight. • Bias identification and mitigation become architectural requirements.
