Data Innovation (Business Service) ()
https://014gc.sharepoint.com/sites/CDO/SitePages/Data-Innovation(1).aspx Data Innovation Data provides us the opportunity to ask and answer key questions about our operations: Who are we helping? How are we helping them? Can we improve this process? Did we have the impact we had hoped for? Who are we leaving behind? Data Innovation is what drives us to find answers to these types of questions and many more. It supports Business Optimization and Analytics for Decision Making. Data Innovation is about finding new ways to answer questions, finding answers to questions we have never been able to answer before, and in finding questions that we have never even thought to ask. Data Innovation is about helping you use Advanced Research Methods, Artificial Intelligence, and Intelligent Automation to learn more about our clients and ourselves. ESDC’s Chief Data Officer Branch can help you understand and use modern Design and Development, as well as Reporting and Analytics Visualization, to make the best choices you can in your role at ESDC.
