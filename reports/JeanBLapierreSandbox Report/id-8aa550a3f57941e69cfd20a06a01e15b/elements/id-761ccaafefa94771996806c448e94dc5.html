O2. Establish Standardized Data Governance and Management Practices for Enterprise Consistency, Compliance and Strategic Alignment ()
O2. Establish standardized data governance and management practices to ensure consistency, compliance, and strategic alignment across the organization. 1. Connecting People to Governance Ensure the right people are engaged at the right time and in the right way to foster a shared understanding and consistent application of data governance policies and processes across the department. This alignment promotes accountability, clarity, and collaboration. 2. Improving Data Maturity and Management Practices Drive the continuous improvement of data maturity by embedding best practices in data management, stewardship, and quality. This strengthens the department’s ability to use data as a strategic asset and supports long-term digital transformation goals.
