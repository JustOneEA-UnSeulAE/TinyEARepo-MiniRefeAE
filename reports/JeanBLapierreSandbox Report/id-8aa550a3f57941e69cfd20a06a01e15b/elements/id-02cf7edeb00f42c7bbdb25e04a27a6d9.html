Quality education (SDG 4) [Goal] ()
Our Early Learning and Child Care (ELCC) and Indigenous ELCC initiatives will help families across Canada access high-quality, affordable, flexible, and inclusive child care. They will also help to improve life outcomes for children and families, and support research projects to fill important data gaps We'll advance this SDG and the Agenda's aim of leaving no one behind through automatic enrolment for the Canada Learning Bond, and raising the age limit to claim it from 20 to 30. These changes will come into effect in 2028 and are expected to benefit over 130,000 children from low-income families each year Through the Supports for Student Learning Program, we'll reduce barriers and provide out of school supports to learners who are at greatest risk of leaving school. Creating equitable opportunities (SDG 10) for learners improves their access to quality education and training, ultimately raising their quality of life
