1.10 Associations are time based DESCRIPTION Associations come in two main flavours: instant or short term, and enduring or long term. Instant, short-term associations are often called transactions, while enduring, long-term associations are often called relationships. Note that associations are often also characterized by spatial attributes in addition to time attributes – when and where did the association occur. RATIONALE • Business interactions occur over time and may start, end, or repeat. • Temporal context is critical for auditability and analytics. IMPLICATION • Include temporal (and often spatial) attributes on associations. • Distinguish transactions from enduring relationships. • Support history and point-in-time analysis.
