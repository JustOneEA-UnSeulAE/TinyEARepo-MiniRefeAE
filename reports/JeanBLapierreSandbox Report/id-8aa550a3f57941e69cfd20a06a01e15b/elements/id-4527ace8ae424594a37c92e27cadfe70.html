WORKING GROUP MANDATES ()
As such, the Working Group is mandated to achieve the following : • Review, provide input and approve the EDFP adoption and enablement Plan. • Deliver on identified activities and deliverables. • Actively participate to in the meetings, bringing expert advice and input. • Raise risks, issues or blockers that could impact the EDFP Operationalization. • Identify and report on all dependencies. • Report on progress of the activities. • Ensure alignment with EDFP strategic objectives to optimize benefits and outcomes. • Foster strategic collaboration with key stakeholders to ensure coherence, synergy and alignment across initiatives and between branches
