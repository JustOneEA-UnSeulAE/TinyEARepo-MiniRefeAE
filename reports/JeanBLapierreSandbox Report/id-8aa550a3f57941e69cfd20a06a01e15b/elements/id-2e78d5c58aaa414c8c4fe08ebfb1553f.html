Core Functionalities ()
The SMMS must provide the following core functionalities: • Publishing: The ability to schedule and publish content on various social media platforms. • Analytics: The ability to perform comprehensive reporting with analytics capabilities to track social media performance metrics. • Monitoring: The ability to monitor, in real time and retroactively, specific topics on social media while tracking key engagement metrics.
