Youth Skills Innovation Directorate/SEB ()
Youth Skills Innovation Directorate/SEB YSID staff are responsible for reporting and coordinating YESS performance data on behalf of the twelve delivery partners. They also act as the YESS lead on the policy design and objectives. The New DCS will enable YSID to meet its reporting obligations under the modernized strategy, as well as respond to emerging policy considerations in a timely and more sophisticated manner.
