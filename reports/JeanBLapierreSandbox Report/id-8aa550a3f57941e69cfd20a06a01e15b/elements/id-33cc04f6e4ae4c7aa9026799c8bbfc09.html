Advance Actions Under Canada's Disability Inclusion Action Plan (Outcome) ()
2.2.1 Primary Strategic Priority In order to align with ESDC's Primary Strategic priority to "Strengthen income security programs and services and work with provinces and territories in reducing poverty," the Government is proceeding with the implementation of a new Canada Disability Benefit to pool its efforts towards building a stronger and more inclusive Canada. This new benefit will improve the standard of living and the quality of life for all Canadians by continuing to advance actions under Canada's Disability Inclusion Action Plan.
