9. Use the active voice Active: The "doer" of the action is right up front. The dog ate the bone. You must name your fear before you can banish it. Passive: The "doer" of the action is near the end of the sentence (or even missing). The bone was eaten. (by the dog?) Named must your fear be before banish it you can. —Yoda quote
