Create a System of Decision Rights and Accountabilities for Data ()
Data Governance and Trust are key concepts for the foundation of sustainable data management. Canadians entrust the Government of Canada and ESDC with their data. They expect that it will be used responsibly and kept secure. Data Governance is about creating a system of decision rights and accountabilities for data that are executed according to agreed-upon models. The trust of the Canadian public is not a given, and it is up to ESDC and other federal government organizations to foster public trust in how the Government of Canada uses their data. Data Governance describeswho can take what actions with what data, under what circumstances, and using what methods. It is also about being open with the public about these processes to improve public trust in Government.
