O1. Enhance Data Quality and Usability; Implementing Rigorous Efficient Processes for Accurate, Consistent, and Fit For Purpose Data ()
O1. Enhance data quality and usability by implementing rigorous yet efficient processes that ensure data is accurate, consistent, and fit for purpose. 1. Build Enterprise Capacity for Data Quality Invest in tools, processes, and training to enable departments to assess, monitor, and continuously improve data quality, ensuring it meets the needs of programs and decision-makers. 2. Architect for Timely Access to High-Quality Data Design systems and infrastructure that prioritize real-time access to the most accurate and relevant data, enabling faster, more informed decisions. 3. Centralized Storage for Trusted Decision-Making Leverage centralized data storage to promote accuracy, efficiency, and trust in data-driven decision-making and process optimization across the organization.
