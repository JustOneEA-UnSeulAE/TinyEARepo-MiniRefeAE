Data visualization process - Text version 6 – Maintain Main data quality Ensure data management Maintain tools and resources Maintain data quality: Continuously monitor and update datasets to ensure accuracy, consistency, and relevance. Implement quality control checks to detect and correct errors or inconsistencies. Continuously verify the reliability of data sources and ensure they remain up to date. Additionally, make the dates of the most recent and upcoming updates clearly visible to clients to enhance transparency and trust. Ensure data management: Adhere to data governance policies, including privacy, security, and compliance requirements. Maintain proper documentation of data sources, transformations, and methodologies. Ensure version control and auditability of datasets is used. Maintain tools and resources: Regularly update and optimize visualization tools and platforms. Provide user support, training, and resources to maximize adoption and usability. Evaluate and integrate new technologies or enhancements to improve performance and insights.
