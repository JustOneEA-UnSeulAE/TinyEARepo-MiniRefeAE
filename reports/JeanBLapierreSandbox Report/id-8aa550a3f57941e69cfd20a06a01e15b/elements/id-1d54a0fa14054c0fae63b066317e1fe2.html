P5 - Data Is Discoverable and Understandable Description Enterprise data assets must be discoverable, well-documented, and understandable through standard metadata, inventories, and catalogs. Rationale The Strategy stresses the importance of data inventories, catalogs, and metadata to enable efficient access, reuse, and evidence-informed decision-making. Impacts • New datasets must be registered in the Enterprise Data Inventory and Catalogue. • Standard metadata (definitions, lineage, steward, sensitivity) is mandatory. • Solutions that do not expose data through enterprise discovery mechanisms are non-compliant. • Reduced reliance on informal knowledge and personal networks to find data.
