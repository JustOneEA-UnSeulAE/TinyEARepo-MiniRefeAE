Undergo An RFP Process ()
This proposal aims to start the procurement activities needed to re-compete the SMMS contract, including posting a request for proposals (RFP). The objective is to give all companies a fair opportunity to provide the SMMS offering and ensure the new contract is awarded in 2026. In going back to market to compete the SMMS contract through a fair, competitive process, ESDC/SC aims to: • maintain SMMS that enable GC departments to publish, schedule, gather analytics, and monitor topics of interest on social media content • support federal institutions in meeting departmental communications objectives and the information needs of Canadians • meet legislative requirements
