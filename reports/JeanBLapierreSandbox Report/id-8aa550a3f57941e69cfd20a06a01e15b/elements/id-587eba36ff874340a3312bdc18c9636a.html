2.1.1 Client Impact The CDB is expected to address the poverty gap for working-aged persons with disabilities and promote social and economic inclusion. Specifically, the CDB is expected to reduce poverty and support the financial security of working-age persons with disabilities, improving the clients Quality of Life domains of prosperity and society. This program is expected to impact up to 520,000 recipients in the first full year of the program. The estimated total including family members is approximately 1.34M.
