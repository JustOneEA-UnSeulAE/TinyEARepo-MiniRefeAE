P1 - Enterprise First [Principle] Provide maximum long-term benefits to the enterprise, while optimizing total costs of ownership and risks ()
P1 - Enterprise First Statement Provide maximum long-term benefits to the enterprise, while optimizing total costs of ownership and risks. Rationale Decisions based on whole government have a greater long-term value than decisions driven by individual programs, projects or organizational units. Implications Client needs must be addressed, and opportunities leveraged to create value by choosing the best solutions in the following order:  - Government of Canada whole-of-government first; then - Department-wide, holistic and horizontal; then - Program or project specific. Solutions must aim to maximize the cumulative business benefits over their entire lifecycle. Investment proposals must be aligned to departmental/enterprise business IT strategies and goals. Business value realization must be tracked and demonstrated on all digital initiatives. Common solutions and shared services that benefit the enterprise are preferred over one-off or siloed solutions. Organizational readiness for adopting new solutions are analyzed and taken into account. Business units must waive their specific preferences to benefit ESDC as a whole. IM/IT priorities must be established by the entire enterprise for the entire enterprise
