1. PIPEDA Consent Requirements ()
Consent Requirements: PIPEDA, like many other data protection laws, requires organizations to obtain individuals’ consent to collect, use, and disclose their personal information. Consent under PIPEDA can be expressed or implied, depending on the circumstances.
