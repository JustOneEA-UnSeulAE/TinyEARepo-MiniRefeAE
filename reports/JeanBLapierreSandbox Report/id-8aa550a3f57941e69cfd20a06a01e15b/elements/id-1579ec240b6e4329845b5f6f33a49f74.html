S-CDS Membership Representation ()
Sub-committee Composition Membership in the Sub-committee on Data Standards (S-CDS) is to include Chief Data Officers (CDOs) and their support staff, with the goal of including representation from at least one small, medium, and large organization, as well as CDO representation from each science and service organizations. S-CDS will also include representation from central agencies.
