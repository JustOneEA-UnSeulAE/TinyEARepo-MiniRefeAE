Next steps Next steps* for implementing the Canada Disability Benefit (CDB) program: 1. Present new benefit amount options to the minister of Finance and the Prime Minister in order to receive a funding decision on the benefit and ongoing funding for delivery of the benefit. 2. Begin regulatory consultation with the disability community on elements outlined in the CDB Act. 3. Continue collaborating with IT on technological solutions and CDB business requirements. 4. Co-design application process with the disability community in order to have an accessible application process. 5. Establish data management systems for information collection and analysis. 6. Work collaboratively with the CRA on data transfer: elements required for CDB administration, data transfer and exchange process. 7. Develop MOU with the CRA. 8. Develop communication and outreach strategies to raise awareness about the CDB. 9. Implement technology solutions for online applications and automated processes. 10. Train staff and stakeholders involved in program implementation. 11. Monitor, evaluate, and iterate the program based on outcomes and user feedback. *Note: These steps are a summary, and further detailed planning and actions will be necessary to fully implement the CDB program.
