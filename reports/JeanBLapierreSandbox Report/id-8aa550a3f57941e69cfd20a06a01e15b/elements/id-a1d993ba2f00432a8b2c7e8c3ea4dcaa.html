CDOC Sub-Committee on Data Standards (S-CDS) Purpose ()
Committee Purpose The purpose of the Sub-committee on Data Standards (S-CDS) is to: • Identify departmental data standards to be adopted, adapted, or newly authored and scaled to the enterprise; • Prioritize the proposed data reference standards and track their development and recommendation through the governance channel, until ultimate endorsement by the ADM Committee on Data and Information; • Facilitate the creation of the data reference standards’ policy instruments with members from Treasury Board Secretariat Office of the Chief Information Officer’s Data and Digital Policy Sector; • Review and recommend data reference standards (including the related policy instrument) to the Chief Data Officer Council.
