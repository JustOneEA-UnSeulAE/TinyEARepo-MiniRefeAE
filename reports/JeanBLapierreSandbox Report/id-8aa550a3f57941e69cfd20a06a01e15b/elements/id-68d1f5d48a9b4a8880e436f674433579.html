10. Use formatting and layout to highlight your information and keep your reader's attention Highlight important text. Use headings and sub-headings. Use relevant and meaningful images when appropriate. Include colour to draw attention and create interest. Leave some white space. Choose your font wisely. Use tables to display data when appropriate. Increase the accessibility* of your texts.
