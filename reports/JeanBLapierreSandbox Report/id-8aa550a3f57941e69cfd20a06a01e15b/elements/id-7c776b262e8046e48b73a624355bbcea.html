Ethical Data Representation (Requirement) ()
Ethical data representation: Ensure fairness and objectivity in data visualization. Avoid misleading visual techniques that could create bias and consider ethical implications of how data is presented and interpreted. Engage relevant stakeholders - such as GBA+ groups for LGBTQ+ data, Indigenous communities for Indigenous data, and subject matter experts for program-specific data to promote inclusivity and responsible, ethical and accurate data representation.
