Federal organizations, departments, & agencies: • Agriculture and Agri-food Canada; • Canadian Heritage • Canada Mortgage and Housing Corporation • Environment and Climate Change Canada; • Employment and Social Development Canada; • Global Affairs Canada; • Indigenous Services Canada; • Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada; • Innovation, Science and Economic Development Canada; • National Research Council; • Natural Resources Canada; • Parks Canada • Each federal organization is responsible to deliver the YESS within its own department and under its Minister’s mandate. They are users of the New DCS in terms of populating administrative and outcome data, and then transferring to ESDC via the new solution. These organizations will benefit from a New DCS by allowing them to have a standardized way to transfer and store the new indicators under the modernized PMF, as well as reduce the administrative burden on them for data reporting and ad-hoc queries
