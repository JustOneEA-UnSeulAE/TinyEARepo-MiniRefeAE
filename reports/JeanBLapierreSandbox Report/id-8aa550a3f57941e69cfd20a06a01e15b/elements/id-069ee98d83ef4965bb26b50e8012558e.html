Potential uses for AI Participants identified many administrative uses for AI, such as drafting and translating text, managing documents and inboxes, and scheduling personnel processes. They saw potential to use AI to generate insights from large public health, environmental, and economic data sets, and to extract information from legal and policy documents and public consultations to inform decisions. They also proposed using AI to make service and legal case processing more efficient.
