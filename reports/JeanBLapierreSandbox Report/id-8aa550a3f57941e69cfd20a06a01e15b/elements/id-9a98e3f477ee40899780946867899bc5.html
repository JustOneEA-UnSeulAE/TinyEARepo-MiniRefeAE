Lack of Real-Time Information (Gap) ()
Lack of Real-Time Information: The absence of real-time access to debtor accounts means that clients cannot view their outstanding debts and loans or payment history instantly. This lack of transparency can lead to confusion, disputes over amounts and payment statuses, and duplicate payments of debts. Currently, the client must contact Canada Revenue Agency and/or Service Canada call centres to obtain a real-time account balance.
