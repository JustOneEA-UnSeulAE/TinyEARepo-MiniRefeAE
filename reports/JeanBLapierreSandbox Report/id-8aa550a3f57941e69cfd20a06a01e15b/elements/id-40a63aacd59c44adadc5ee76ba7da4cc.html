Benefits and Integrated Services Branch (BISB) (Stakeholder) ()
Individual Payment and Services On Demand (IPSOD) within BISB is responsible for overall accountability to the service design, development and implementation of the program, which includes design of operational implementation, processing, training, and quality assurance, reporting. Call Centre Directorate within BISB will provide live support to callers enquiring about the program and/or over the phone support to persons applying for the program and/or enquiring about the status of an application.
