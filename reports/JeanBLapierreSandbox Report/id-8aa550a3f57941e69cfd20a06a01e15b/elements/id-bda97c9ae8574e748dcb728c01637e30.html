Trust in a digital world (Value) ()
In this digital world, Canadians must be able to trust that their privacy is protected, that their data will not be misused, and that companies operating in this space communicate in a simple and straightforward manner with their users. This trust is the foundation on which our digital and data-driven economy will be built. The 10 principles of the Charter will help guide the federal government's work to help address challenges and leverage Canada's unique talents and strengths in order to harness the power of digital and data transformation.
