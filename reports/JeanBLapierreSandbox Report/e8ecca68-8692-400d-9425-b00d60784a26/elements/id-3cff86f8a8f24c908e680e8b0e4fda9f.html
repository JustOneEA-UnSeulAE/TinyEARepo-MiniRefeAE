Findable: Make your data easy to locate Metadata is key: Always include detailed, standardized metadata—clear descriptions, tags, and keywords—that improve searchability. Persistent identifiers: Use permanent identifiers (like digital object identifiers) to ensure your data is easily retrievable in the long term. Publish data in repositories: Upload your data to trusted and searchable platforms (such as the Government of Canada's open data platform: https://open.canada.ca/en) so that it's accessible to stakeholders and the public.
