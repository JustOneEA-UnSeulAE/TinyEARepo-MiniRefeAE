Zero Trust Capabilities The Digital Age does not give us the luxury of time to meet the requirements using traditional network-based solutions, or individualized, interface, or per client, vendor, or supplier-level integrations; nor can we predict what changes it will bring. Zero Trust helps prevent lengthy audits in order to meet organizational needs. Zero Trust provides organizations with a modern information security paradigm able to meet the capabilities that can support those requirements. Figure 4 describes the key capabilities.
