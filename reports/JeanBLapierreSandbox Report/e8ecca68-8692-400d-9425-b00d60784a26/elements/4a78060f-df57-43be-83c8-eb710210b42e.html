Business Requirements ()
Definition: Any protection or compliance requirement associated with a business activity that can be satisfied by security controls. Business needs for security are derived from laws (e.g., Employment Insurance Act, Financial Administration Act), policies (e.g., Policy on Financial Management, Information and Reporting), and any other regulatory instruments such as directives and standards governing GC business activities. Business needs for security can also be derived from departmental missions, objectives, priorities, the need to preserve the organization’s image and reputation, and various obligations that may have been contracted. (ITSG-33)
