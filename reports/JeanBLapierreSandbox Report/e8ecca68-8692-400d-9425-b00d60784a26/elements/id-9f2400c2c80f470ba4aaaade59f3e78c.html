Worker Training ()
We have an opportunity to not just support Canadians, but grow their potential. Working with the provinces and territories, the Government will make the largest investment in Canadian history in training for workers. This will include by: • Supporting Canadians as they build new skills in growing sectors; • Helping workers receive education and accreditation; • And strengthening workers’ futures, by connecting them to employers and good jobs, in order to grow and strengthen the middle class.
