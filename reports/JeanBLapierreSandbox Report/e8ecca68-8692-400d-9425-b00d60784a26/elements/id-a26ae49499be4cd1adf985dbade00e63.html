Risk - Application Decommissioning (Assessment) ()
Risk Description: There is a risk that the decommissioning of application do not respect the established processes regarding information management or that the server remains active due to improper respect of procedures. Impact Description: Unused, yet active servers, will result in a reduction of benefit realisation. Added/delayed work will have an impact on schedule and project costs.
