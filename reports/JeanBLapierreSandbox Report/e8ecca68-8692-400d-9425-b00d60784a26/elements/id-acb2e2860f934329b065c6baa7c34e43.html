1.2.6. Transformation and Business Delivery Modernization (BDM) The CDO is a part of a larger departmental and governmental transformation agenda. In particular, the largest department transformation program, Business Delivery Modernization (BDM), has requirements that CDO will work toward collaboratively to fulfill while keeping line of sight to enterprise outcomes. Initially identified BDM future state capabilities and enablers will be coordinated synergistically with CDO data foundations. This will be equally true for other transformation activities.
