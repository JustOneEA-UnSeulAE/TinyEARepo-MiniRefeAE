"Simple Site" (Meaning) ()
"Simple Sites" in SharePoint 2010 that contain only Lists and/or Libraries. Owners of Simple Sites will be notified at least four weeks before their migration date and, in most cases, will have a handful of items to address in their site before and after migration to SharePoint Online.
